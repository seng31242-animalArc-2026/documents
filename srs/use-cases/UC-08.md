# UC-08 — Conduct Treatment

Use Case ID: UC-08

Use Case Name:
Conduct Treatment

Actors:
Volunteer Doctor

Preconditions:
- Doctor must be assigned to the case.

Postconditions:
- Treatment details are recorded successfully.

Main Flow:
1. Volunteer opens assigned case.
2. Reviews animal condition.
3. Conducts treatment.
4. Enters medicine and treatment notes.
5. System saves treatment history.

Alternative Flow:
- If medicine stock is unavailable, system shows warning.

Business Rules:
- Every treatment must be linked to a valid case.
